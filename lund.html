lundberg family farms

digital advertising
illustration
web design

Team: Haberman
Creative Direction: Jesse Ross
Lead Designer: Kate Nelson

Organic grain company Lundberg Family Farms approached marketing firm Haberman with the challenge of creating a summer campaign to promote the benefit of eating whole grains in a fresh, funny, and digital-savvy way.

The solution? Silly, shareable illustrations of what happens when a person is "undergrained", digital ads (including special videos from YouTube star Anna Akana), and an interactive site. With the collaboration of the Haberman team, I created digital materials and original illustrations to bring this campaign to life.
