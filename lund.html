LUNDBERG FAMILY FARMS


digital campaign, illustration


For their summer 2016 campaign, organic grain company Lundberg Family Farms wanted to show the benefit of eating whole grains in a fresh, funny, and digital-savvy way. The solution? Silly, shareable illustrations of what happens when a person is "undergrained", digital ads (including special videos from YouTube star Anna Akana), and an interactive site. With the team at Haberman, I created digital materials and original illustrations to bring this campaign to life.

beagrainiac.com

» This campaign was specifically aimed at a younger audience who is just starting to pay attention to cooking and eating healthily, and want accessible information on how to do so.

» For the illustrations, I mixed flat colors and geometric shapes with hand-drawn elements for a fun, bright aesthetic that fit with the rest of the campaign

SOCIAL + DIGITAL EXTENSION


Campaign included social media and digital advertising, including targeting placements for Pandora and Spotify.
