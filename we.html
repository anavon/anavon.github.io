world endeavors

print campaign
editorial
brand implementation

World Endeavors is a Minneapolis-based company that offers study, intern, and volunteer opportunities in over 30 countries. To spread the word about the growing company, World Endeavors commissioned me to create new print materials to coincide with their rebrand and website update.

These items included business cards, posters, standing banners for fairs and events, informative brochures for specific programs, and a 40-page lookbook to showcase participant photography and inspire future travelers.
