THE IDEA CHALLENGE


digital and print campaign, illustration


The Idea Challenge is a yearly event that encourages entrepreneurs to come up with innovative ideas to improve the community. The winning applicants receive funding and assistance to turn their idea into a successful product, service, or process. I designed the 2015 campaign to be friendly, accessible, and eye-catching, while using the existing logo and colors of the Idea Challenge brand and emphasizing the idea of community. Ads were launched in a web page takeover, facebook, and on the Idea Challenge website, as well as print publications.

>> One of the challenges of this campaign was aiming at the right demographic, which had been a problem in previous years. The final solution of flat illustration and bright colors is accessible to all but skews more towards a younger, tech-focused audience than previous campaigns.

>> To highlight and explain the different types of entries more in-depth, I created separate product, service, and process posts for social media, as well as illustrated buttons for the Idea Challenge website that would give more information on past participants.
