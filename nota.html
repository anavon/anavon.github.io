NOTA (NONE OF THE ABOVE)


art direction, editorial, illustration


NOTA (None of the Above) is the award-winning literature, art, and music publication at University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire. Starting out as a channel for students to publish creative work, NOTA has become a bridge between the university and the city of Eau Claire, hosting public events like Open Reads, concerts, and book release parties. During my time working for NOTA, I held the positions of graphic designer and assistant art director and have worked on the production of three books and numerous smaller publications and materials.

» NOTA has existed at UW-Eau Claire since the 1980s, and has grown from a small-scale newspaper to a professionally-printed and bound book. Through student funding and wide community support, the publication has been able to remain free and accessible to everyone.

» 1500 - 2000 books are printed and distributed each semester throughout the Eau Claire community. There are also smaller prose-only 'pocket notas', buttons, stickers, bookmarks, and a local music CD produced with each book.

» Submissions are open to everyone and determined by popular vote (art) or a juried committee (literature). During my time at NOTA, the average number of submissions each semester doubled, with over 100 people submitting their work to each category.
