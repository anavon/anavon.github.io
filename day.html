DAIYA


digital content marketing, html newsletter


Daiya is one of the leading producers of plant-based dairy alternatives, but they didn't want to only be seen as an alternative. Through a redesign and refresh of Daiya's web presence and social channels, my colleagues at Haberman and I reintroduced Daiya to the food world not as a food alternative, but as a delicious, healthy food that even non-vegans would choose.

» Although the website design was mostly completed when I started at Haberman, I designed the entire newsletter integration, including the sign up page on the website, the html newsletter itself, and the monthly content inside each edition.

» To create the delicious food images for the Daiya social posts, I helped prepare and direct in-house photoshoots. These photograph-based posts make up most of Daiya's social presence to emphasize the quality and variety of their food, but I also occasionally created illustrations or graphics to integrate the line-art style of the website.
