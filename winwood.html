WINWOOD PROPERTIES


logo design, branding


Winwood Properties, LLC is a real estate business based in Lake Mills, Wisconsin that specializes on renovating houses to resell or rent. I was commissioned to create a logo and brand identity for the new business that communicated quality and renewal.

>> The client originally wanted to incorporate a maple leaf into the logo. This idea was later vetoed by both myself and Winwood in favor of the simpler house logo, but the warm gold color scheme stayed.
