amazon big game styleguide

styleguide creation
branding
art direction

Amazon's Big Game is a sitewide event that promotes deals and special offers on Amazon.com during the Super Bowl. For the 2017 event, I created the styleguide and branding that was used on all Amazon graphics, and directed other designers working on this project.

For this styleguide, I pushed the typically minimal Amazon style towards something bold and active. With bright contrasting colors and gritty texture, I used the visual language of football and sport without incorporating too much literal imagery to let the product photos speak for themselves.
