ALBATROSS TRAVEL


branding, web design


Albatross Travel was born partially from my desire to create a full responsive website on my own, and partially from my frustration at the confusing, cluttered user experience of many current travel-related sites. I designed the Albatross site to act as a search tool for vacations but also as a personalized content feed and trip-planning space if a user signs in.

>> Albatrosses can fly over 10,000 km at a time with minimal effort due to their streamlined wings, so the seabird is an appropriate symbol for a company focused on travel and efficiency. The color scheme is simple and relaxing, but bright enough to stand out against photographic backgrounds.

>> Although non-members would still be able to search trips and read blog posts, members are able to bookmark trips and posts and receive content curated specifically for their travel needs.
